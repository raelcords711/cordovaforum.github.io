Samsung Galaxy Note 8

The Samsung Galaxy Note 8 (marketed as Samsung Galaxy Note8) is an Android phablet smartphone designed, developed and marketed by Samsung Electronics. Unveiled on 23 August 2017, it is the successor to the Samsung Galaxy Note 7. Available case colors, depending on region, are: Midnight Black, Maple Gold, Deep Sea Blue and Orchid Gray.[3] The stylus or S Pen has pressure levels at 4096. The pen tip has a diameter of 0.7 mm, for writing. The pen also includes the Bixby vision feature.
